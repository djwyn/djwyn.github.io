DJ WYN: Mobile DJ & MC

The best Karaoke DJ in San Jose has expanded his services to include mixing dance music as a mobile DJ and MC!

Aside from private events like weddings and corporate parties, DJ WYN has also mixed music in a variety of public venues and in front of a variety of audiences.

Highlights include:
  • Cruise Ship DJ on the MV Explorer (Caribbean)
  • Bogart's in Sunnyvale
  • Number 1 Broadway in Los Gatos
  • El Torito in Santa Clara
  • Chevy's Restaurants (multiple locations)
  • Homestead Lanes in Cupertino

Specialties

  • Events
  • Weddings
  • Corporate Events
  • Weekly and Monthly Public Shows
  • Music
  • Top 40
  • Old Skool
  • Latin Dance

DJ WYN is the resident DJ at Homestead Lanes at 20990 Homestead Rd., Cupertino, California. He can be seen every Sunday evening, 8 pm-midnight.

DJ WYN also shares the DJ booth every Friday at El Torito in Santa Clara, 6 pm - midnight.

DJ WYN hosts the monthly salsa dance party and teaches the beginner salsa lesson on Saturdays at El Torito in Santa Clara, 7-11:30 pm.
